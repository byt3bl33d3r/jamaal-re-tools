Part of volatility.plugins.addrspaces.legacyintel View In Hierarchy

Known subclasses: volatility.plugins.addrspaces.legacyintel.IA32PagedMemoryPae

Legacy x86 non PAE address space (to use specify --use_old_as)

We accept an optional arg called dtb to force us to use a specific dtb. If not provided, we try to find it from our base AS, and failing that we search for it.
